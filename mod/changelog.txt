Version: 1.1.0
Date: 2026-03-09
  Major Features:
    - Variable speed of belt-like entities in a section depending on the ratio of engine power to the power required by the section; you can trade speed for section size.
  Features:
    - Belt-like entities with different speeds no longer have different names; a custom status is now applied that reflects their speed.
    - Section divider recipe, item, and entity prototypes and their icons are auto-generated from their base belts.
  Optimizations:
    - Belt-engine state is re-evaluated faster when the player builds an electric pole in its range.
    - Drastically improved performance of processing belt engine states by 5-6x.
    - Improved performance of building, rotating, and removing belts and belt engines for large belt-like entity sections.
  Bugfixes:
    - Blueprint now correctly interacts with belts.
    - Upgrade and deconstruction planners now correctly interact with belts.
    - Upgrade planner can be applied to belt engines.
    - Case where a T junction is formed at a turn belt or in similar cases and belt sections were not recalculating.
    - Error when belt-like sections were not correctly re-evaluated when their configuration changed due to building, rotating, or removing adjacent belts.
    - Error when belt-like sections were not correctly re-evaluated when underground belts were built.
